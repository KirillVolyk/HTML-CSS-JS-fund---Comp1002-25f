Tokyo Denki Kagaku Kōgyō(TDK)

TDK Corporation is a Japanese multinational electronics company founded in 1935 as Tokyo Denki Kagaku Kōgyō (Tokyo Electric Chemical Industry). It originally specialized in ferrite, a magnetic material, and later became famous worldwide for its cassette tapes and recording media. Today, TDK has shifted focus to producing electronic components such as capacitors, inductors, sensors, and power supplies, serving industries from consumer electronics to automotive and industrial systems.
