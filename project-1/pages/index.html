About: TDK-SA-C90

Before going about this cassette, let's start by: What is a cassette? Cassette is a cartridge that holds magnetic tape used to record and play audio. Popular from the 1970s through the 1990s.

Now, what is SA-C90? The TDK SA‑C90 wasn’t just another cassette — it was a high‑bias audio tool designed for people who cared about sound quality. The “SA” stood for Super Avilyn, TDK’s advanced magnetic coating that gave sharper highs and richer lows compared to standard tapes. The “C90” meant 90 minutes of recording time (45 minutes per side), making it perfect for full albums or carefully crafted mixtapes.
